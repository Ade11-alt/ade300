ANGER

Anger is a strong feeling of displeasure or hostility. It can be a response to perceived threats, injustice, or frustration.

It can be expressed in various ways, from mild irritation to intense rage.

Understanding and managing anger is important for emotional well-being.

Surprise

This is a neutral emotion that occurs when something unexpected happens. It can be positive (a pleasant surprise), negative (a sudden threat), or neutral (a curious event). Surprise usually lasts only a few seconds before evolving into another emotion.
